05Prompt tuning journey

The first version worked. The tuned version converted — and earned trust.

Going live in a week is fast. Going live well took three deliberate adjustments — each one small in word count, large in outcome.

v1 Before — generic, extractive
Hi! How can I help you today? We offer tax services for individuals and businesses. Feel free to ask anything.
v2 After — scoped, consultative, in language
Halo! Saya bantu konsultasi pajak dan pembukuan untuk pribadi dan bisnis di Finfloo. Lagi butuh bantuan untuk apa — SPT tahunan, laporan keuangan, atau pajak perusahaan? Saya akan bantu arahkan ke solusi yang paling sesuai kondisi Anda.
01
Scoped to real services
Agent speaks only to bookkeeping, tax filing, and financial reporting — what Finfloo actually does. No vague "we do business stuff."
02
Empathic over extractive
Shifted from aggressively collecting visitor data to a consultative, domain-expert posture. The agent asks to understand, not to harvest — mirroring how a knowledgeable tax advisor would engage a new client.
03
Scam & fraud guardrails
Detects and deflects social engineering, impersonation, and attempts to manipulate the agent — protecting Finfloo's reputation and visitor trust.
